How does it work?

Step 1

Choose the item you are looking for or signup for a different one

Step 2:

After choosing an item, you will be directed to choose a store of choice

Step 3:

If the item is not in stock, you can signup to be notified when it is

Step 4:

To signup for a non predefined item: Enter your email address and the Amazon ASIO Code as shown

Step 5:

Finally, make sure to enter your Discord Username to get notified on Discord
